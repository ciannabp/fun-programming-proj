Plot 1

National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent to Adult Health

What are the most popular breakfast items for 7th-12th Graders? Participants were asked to indicate all of the breakfast items that they consume on a regular basis. Unsurprisingly, both milk and cereal were the most commonly chosen answers while coffee, tea, and snack foods were the least commonly consumed.

Plot 2

National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent to Adult Health

What is the average screen time by grade iterated across race? Participants were asked to indicate separately how many minutes per week they watched television, videos, and played videogames. We created a composite measure for general weekly screen time and found the average for each grade and dominant race category. From this we can see that for White participants, screen time gernerally decreases as grade increases. However, this pattern was not consistent for other race categories and screen time remained fairly consistent across grades for Black and African American participants.

Plot 3

National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent to Adult Health

Do insomnia symptoms predict depressed mood across adolescence? Participants reported the frequency and intensity of insomnia symptoms (Likert scale 0-4), depressed mood (Likert scale 0-4), and average number of sleep hours in the past week. Model 1 posits that increased insomnia scores subsequently predict increased depressed mood. Model 2 posits that increased insomnia scores and sleep hours together predict increased depressed mood. The plot above shows the amount of variance explained for each model by grade. Based on the results, there appears to be significantly more variance explained in Model 2 as compared to Model 1 for depressed mood outcomes.
